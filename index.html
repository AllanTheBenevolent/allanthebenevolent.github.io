Just A Moment

“Just a Moment” previously named NGN(Nice Guy/Girl Nation) is a project dedicated to spreading kindness and appreciation to one another. Feel good while doing good. It takes just a moment in an individual’s life to make a difference in another. The purpose of this project is to encourage individuals of this generation to take a step back from their busy, fast-paced lives and take a moment to appreciate what they have and what they are able to do. Kindness is the language we speak and we believe that a little generosity here and there can create a positive ripple effect to the community as a whole.
I was the founder and content creator for Just A Moment. I started at the end of my freshman year as an undergrad and it gain traction quite rapidly. I lead various successful projects and campaign to promote kindness through this organization. I successfully achieved a partnership with a dance consulting firm which soon decided to adapt and run the project with various volunteers.

  • Client: UC San Diego
  • Date: September 2013 - June 2015
  • Service: Non-Profit

Global Ties Town and Country Team

Global TIES - Teams in Engineering Service is an innovative humanitarian engineering program of the Jacobs School of Engineering at UC San Diego.
Global TIES teams give students an opportunity to apply their skills in a real-world setting while learning the role that engineering and technology can play in solving the local and global problems.
I was part of this program for around 2 years and served as a team lead for one of the sub projects. The team I was in was called Town and Country Learning Center. We were in charge of providing a to educate and enhance the environment for the students that attend the learning center. I was in charge of two mini projects within the group. The first project was a music application that incorporated an onsite piano that taught students basic key skills and allowed them to showcase their work. The 2nd project was a sensor system to help notify students when they would need to water and care for their plants in their gardens. I’ve logged and documented all my work for continuity. I no longer am part of the project and have passed on my work to the next generation of students taking this program.

  • Client: Town and Country Learning Center
  • Date: March 2013- June 2015
  • Service: Humanitarian Engineering, Non Profit

MASA(Multi Asian Student Association)

UCSD MASA is a social-cultural organization dedicated to educating the community of the rich diversity of the Asian student body of UCSD. It’s noted as one of the largest student run organization on the UCSD campus. MASA has been established as an all inclusive organization since 1999 and is continue to strive to grow their ever large network.
I’ve served 2 years on board and am now the current Vice President of this lovely organization. I’ve managed and facilitated various large community events hosted by this organization. I managed a team of 19 other board members to create luxurious fun filled events for the student body at UCSD. I’ve also been the production manager for one of the largest student run dance competition in California. I’ve completely revamped and improved the organization’s staff structural system. I also sparked the jump to incorporate social media and technology to further improve membership and functionality of the organizations. I am currently managing a small project team that is in charge of developing the organizations new and improved website. I’ve exponentially grew the amount of member participant in our events. This organization is one of my proudest contributions to the UCSD community.
