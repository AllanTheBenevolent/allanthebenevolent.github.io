Just A Moment

It takes just a moment in an individual’s life to make a difference in another. The purpose of this project is to encourage individuals of this generation to take a step back from their busy, fast-paced lives and take a moment to appreciate what they have and what they are able to do. Tastebuddies


Tastebuddies is a web application that I designed and developed for a HCI(Human Computer Interaction) design class. I managed a group of three students to develop a design prototype of the application within 10 weeks. The application’s purpose was inspired by the lack of motivation and desire for students to cook, since we believed that cooking was an essential part of life and it’s a skill that many college students lack and may benefit from learning. Through the course we did many observations and design mock ups to come up with the applications core values and functions. After many prototyping and researching we developed an application that takes in an input of ingredients and returns a list of recipes the user can create with the ingredients inputted. We also created a gamification aspect to the application by allowing users to collect “tastebuddies” or characters as they continue to use the application and cook more recipes. The concept was to have tastebuddies become companions to cook with and be the encouragement users need to pursue the culinary arts.

I was the one who introduced the idea of cooking as a need for this project and became assigned as the product manager. The development and design of the entire application was an enriching experience. I learned a lot in the technical side as well as learning how to be a product manager when dealing with design with users and addressing user and market needs.

Β Δ Σ

ΒΔΣ or Beta Delta Sigma, which stands for “Best Dim Sum” was originally a group created for entertainment purposes and just inside joke towards the Greek community. However the letters and mission concept was quite intriguing to peers and quickly gained traction and started to become a larger entity. Having the concept originally being a play on the Greek letters it soon developed into a philanthropist group. Dim Sum’s literal translation; “touch the heart” soon became a motto for the group. The motto that inspired every member to touch the hearts of all those who cross their paths and remember to always strive to be an influence to others in some positive manner.

I was one of the four founders who never expected such a concept to gain a big traction. With the support of eager friends and colleagues, the group grew exponentially and became a popular social group that was dedicated to making a change in individuals’ lives. We did various small charities and events for the community and continue to reach out to individuals’ heart to share the culture of Dim Sum as well as the need for humanity to love one another. This organization was one of the cleverest concepts I’m proud of creating.
